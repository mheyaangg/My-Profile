About Me

"Hi, I'm Marrian Angelah Florece! I appreciate the simple joys in life—soft aesthetics, warm atmospheres, and anything that brings a sense of comfort. Whether it's technology, creativity, or just a peaceful moment, I love finding beauty in the little things. This space is a reflection of that, a place to share and enjoy cozy vibes."

Skills & Expertise

I specialize in anime, cyberpunk, and painterly digital art, focusing on dreamy compositions with vibrant pink, purple, and blue tones. With strong skills in character design, lighting, and blending techniques, I create expressive and atmospheric illustrations that bring depth and emotion to my work.
